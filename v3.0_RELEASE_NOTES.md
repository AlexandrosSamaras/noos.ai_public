# Noos.AI v3.0 Release Notes

We are excited to introduce Noos.AI v3.0, a major update focused on personalization and deep workflow integration. This release brings the power of AI directly into your writing process and connects your insights with your favorite tools.

## 🚀 New Features

### 1. True Persona Cloning (Train Your Voice)
Stop sounding like a generic AI. Noos.AI can now analyze your own writing samples to understand your unique tone, style, and vocabulary.
*   **How it works:** Go to the Options page, paste 3 samples of your writing (emails, messages, posts), and click "Analyze & Save My Voice".
*   **Result:** All "Rewrite", "Reply", and "Follow-up" actions will now automatically use your personal writing style.

### 2. Ghost Input & Deep Integration
AI shouldn't just live in a popup. We've integrated Noos.AI directly into your browser's input fields.
*   **Rewrite & Improve:** Select any text you've typed in a text box (email, social media, etc.), right-click, and choose "Rewrite & Improve". The AI will polish your text and **replace it in-place** instantly.
*   **Draft Reply:** Select an incoming message or email, right-click, and choose "Draft Reply". Noos.AI will generate a context-aware response ready for you to use.

### 3. Custom Persona Manager
Beyond your own voice, you can now create specific personas for different needs.
*   Create personas like "Professional Marketer", "ELI5 Explainer", or "Strict Editor".
*   Manage them easily from the new Options page interface.

### 4. Notion Integration
Seamlessly save your AI insights.
*   Connect your Notion account in the Options page (API Key & Page ID).
*   Send summaries, explanations, or any AI result directly to your chosen Notion page with a single click.

### 5. Enhanced Context Menu
*   New **"AI Search Selection"**: Select text and instantly perform an AI-powered search to get synthesized answers.
*   New **"Open in LLM"**: Quickly open your selected text or prompt in ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini for a deeper dive.

### 6. Premium UI & Visual Excellence
Experience a significant visual leap with our new "Context-First" design language:
*   **Rich Markdown Rendering:** All AI results (summaries, replies, search) now support full Markdown formatting for beautiful, structured reading.
*   **Fluid Animations:** Smooth `fadeInUp` entrance animations for content and interactive micro-animations throughout.
*   **Visual Copy Feedback:** Get instant confirmation when copying results with our new animated checkmark feedback.
*   **Glassmorphism Overhaul:** A refined, consistent glassmorphic aesthetic across all sites and themes.

### 7. Magic Pointer v2 (Spatial AI)
Our most advanced feature yet. Noos.AI now has "eyes" to see the web exactly as you do.
*   **Intelligent Hover:** Hold `Alt` and watch the AI target elements in real-time with a cyan glow.
*   **Alt + Drag Selection:** Don't just click—drag a rectangle over any part of the screen to isolate it for analysis.
*   **YouTube Transcript Synthesis:** Use the Magic Pointer on any YouTube video to instantly analyze its spoken content alongside the visual frames.
*   **Scanning Animations:** A premium radar-pulse animation provides instant feedback during analysis.

## 🛠 Improvements & Fixes
*   **Activation Fix:** Resolved the "V.Fail(429)" error that some users experienced during license verification.
*   **Performance:** Optimized "Ghost Input" for faster in-place text replacement.
*   **UI/UX:** Refreshed Options page with a cleaner layout for managing integrations and personas.
*   **Stability:** Improved error handling for network requests and backend services.
